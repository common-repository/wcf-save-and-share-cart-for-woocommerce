WCF Save & Share Cart for WooCommerce let's you save and share your cart with your friends.

== Description ==

WCF Save & Share Cart for WooCommerce enable saving, sharing, 
and restoring cart via an auto-generated link with your friends.
It can be utilized for sharing products directly from any WooCommerce store or saving 
item lists as Wishlist or Saved for Later. This helps to reduce the time spent searching, grouping 
products together in order to buy.
